If the EU AI Act August 2026 enforcement creates audits of deployers (not just providers), demand for inventory/governance tools at SME price points will spike. This could enter the top 10 in the next 2–4 weeks as enforcement signals clarify.

3. **AI token cost attribution for non-engineering buyers** (adjacent to DevROI) — the CFO version of DevROI. DevROI targets engineering managers and VPs; the broader financial visibility problem exists for CFOs and heads of finance who want to see AI tool spend across ALL departments (not just engineering), categorized by business outcome. This is the "AI spend management" category that is not yet served by a focused product. A broader version of DevROI targeting finance teams at $20M–$200M ARR companies could score 79–82/100 and would not be a duplicate of the engineering-focused DevROI.
